BITCRYPT is a deck based dungeon crawling game with an emphasis on digital collectables and glitch art.

- Traverse the encrypted digital realm and acquire distinctive cards to forge a formidable deck.
- Each card boasts unparalleled abilities and fosters potent combinations when strategically paired with others.
- Secure new cards through triumphant victories against adversaries and formidable bosses, or by procuring them via the designated marketplace.
